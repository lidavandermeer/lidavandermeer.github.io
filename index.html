ABOUT ME

I am an innovative connector studying Cognitive Science and Media Studies at UC Berkeley. I am passionate about building unique and uplifting relationships and the empowerment of authenticity.

I am dedicated to problem solving and working towards creating more proficient solutions through logic, and believe different perspectives and collaboration with others can lead to the best solutions. My communication style is straightforward and empathetic, allowing me to quickly cultivate mutually respectful relationships that drive productivity. I am a strategic thinker who works diligently to elevate and promote a brand in the strongest possible light. I have been recognized by my network for my innate ability to quickly understand a brand and their target market, thus bringing a powerful perspective to the brand development process.
