ABOUT ME

I’m a product problem expert grounded in the long term vision. My favorite products to design are machine learning based enterprise solutions.

I view myself as a user advocate above all else, vetting every decision to ensure the integrity of a relevant, adoptable solution. I prioritize solutions to user pain with strategic and tactical responses that benefit both product managers and agile development teams. I am intentional about leveraging my empathy and leadership qualities for efficient and energizing communication with internal stakeholders - from engineers to executive management.

Beyond my day to day, I enjoy synthesizing my experiences into writing to contribute to the online product design community. Outside of work, I love road tripping to remote areas and am currently DIYing a camper van conversion.
